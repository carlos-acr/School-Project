About

One of America’s most beloved restaurants, Run the Dish has welcomed guests to enjoy its Italian cuisine, warm hospitality and unparalleled service for over two decades. Chef Tony’s renowned restaurant, delights the senses with award winning contemporary Italian cuisine. It is inspired by the seasons and accompanied by world class wines, we offer private dining and a warm welcoming Bar & Lounge where guests enjoy creative cocktails, dining, and sumptuous late night desserts. The distinctive setting boasts contemporary furnishings and finishes atop the restaurant’s grand architecture.
